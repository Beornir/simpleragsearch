# Grandma's Famous Lasagna

Okay so this is my grandmother's recipe that she brought from Italy. DO NOT CHANGE ANYTHING.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 lb Italian sausage
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 28 oz crushed tomatoes (San Marzano ONLY)
- 6 oz tomato paste
- 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 15 oz ricotta cheese
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 lb mozzarella, shredded
- 3/4 cup Parmesan, grated
- 12 lasagna noodles

## Instructions

1. Brown the beef and sausage in a large pot. Drain fat.
2. Add onion and garlic, cook 5 min.
3. Add tomatoes, paste, seasonings. Simmer 45 min.
4. Mix ricotta, egg, and parsley in a bowl.
5. Cook noodles according to package.
6. Layer in 9x13 pan: sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella. Repeat 3 times.
7. Top with remaining sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
8. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 min. Let rest 15 min before serving.
