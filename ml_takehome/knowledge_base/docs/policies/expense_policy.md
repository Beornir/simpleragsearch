# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2024
**Department:** Finance
**Status:** Active

## General Rules

1. All expenses must be submitted through **Expensify** within **30 days** of the transaction.
2. Receipts required for all expenses over **$25**.
3. Manager approval required for single expenses over **$500**.
4. VP approval required for single expenses over **$2,000**.

## Travel

- **Flights**: Economy class for domestic. Premium economy for international flights over 6 hours. Business class requires VP pre-approval.
- **Hotels**: Up to $250/night domestic, $350/night international. Use the corporate Navan portal for bookings.
- **Meals during travel**: Up to $75/day per person. No alcohol on company card.
- **Ground transport**: Uber/Lyft preferred. Rental cars require pre-approval.

## Team Events

- **Team dinners**: Up to $100/person including tax and tip.
- **Team offsites**: Budget of $300/person/day. Must be pre-approved by Director+.
- **Conference attendance**: Registration + travel. Maximum 2 conferences per year per employee. Must be approved by manager.

## Software and Subscriptions

- Under $50/month: Manager approval via Slack (screenshot the approval).
- $50-500/month: Submit through Jira IT ticket for procurement review.
- Over $500/month: Requires Finance and Legal review. Contact procurement@meridian.tech.

## Reimbursement Timeline

Approved expenses are reimbursed within **5 business days** via direct deposit to your payroll account.

## Non-Reimbursable

- Personal travel extensions during business trips
- Alcohol (except at company-approved events)
- First-class travel without pre-approval
- Home internet bills (covered under home office stipend instead)
- Gym memberships (use the wellness benefit instead)
