# PTO Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2023
**Department:** Human Resources

## Time Off Allowances

All full-time employees receive PTO based on level:

- **IC1-IC3**: 18 days per year, up to 3 days carryover
- **IC4-IC5**: 22 days per year, up to 5 days carryover
- **Managers**: 22 days per year, up to 5 days carryover
- **Directors and above**: 30 days per year, up to 10 days carryover

## How to Request

Submit all PTO requests through **BambooHR** at least 3 business days in advance for requests of 3+ days.

## Blackout Periods

Engineering team: December 20 - January 1 (code freeze period)

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 8 sick days per year. These do not carry over. Doctor's note required for absences over 5 consecutive days.

## Parental Leave

Meridian offers 12 weeks paid parental leave for primary caregivers and 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.

Contact: hr@meridian.tech
