# Remote Work Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2024
**Department:** People Operations
**Status:** Active

## Work Model

Meridian operates a **hybrid-flexible** model:

- **Engineering, Data, ML teams**: Fully remote. No mandatory office days.
- **Sales, Customer Success**: 3 days/week in office (Tue/Wed/Thu).
- **All other teams**: 2 days/week in office (Wed required, one additional day team's choice).

## Home Office Stipend

All employees receive a **$1,500 one-time home office stipend** upon hire, plus **$500/year** for ongoing equipment. Submit receipts through Expensify within 30 days of purchase.

## Co-working Spaces

If you don't have a suitable home office, the company covers co-working memberships up to **$300/month**. Pre-approval required from your manager. WeWork and Industrious are preferred vendors (direct billing available).

## International Remote Work

Employees may work from outside the US for up to **4 weeks per calendar year** with manager approval. Tax implications are the employee's responsibility. Notify HR at least 2 weeks before travel. Countries under US sanctions are not permitted.

## Communication Expectations

- **Core hours**: 10am-3pm Pacific Time. All remote employees must be available during core hours regardless of timezone.
- **Response times**: Slack messages within 2 hours during core hours. Email within 24 hours.
- **Camera on**: Cameras should be on for meetings with 5 or fewer participants. Optional for larger meetings.

## Equipment

Standard issue for all employees:
- MacBook Pro 14" (M3 Pro, 18GB RAM) — Engineering
- MacBook Air 15" (M3, 16GB RAM) — Non-engineering
- One external monitor (request through IT)
- Keyboard and mouse of choice (within $200 budget)

For ML engineers who need GPU access, see the Cloud Compute policy or request a workstation through #it-requests.
